About Family Care Dental Clinic

Family Care Dental Clinic is a family owned clinic located in front of Dapitan City Hall. It is operated and owned by Doctor Glenn Mohametano and his wife Ivy Mohametano. The Clinic has been operating since 2018 giving people of Zamboanga del Norte the outmost dental services it can offer by giving their client the guaranteed experience and enjoy every such aspect with every service.

Family Care Dental Clinic is your hub for comprehensive and highly specialized dental treatments with the state of the art equipments for dental services.
